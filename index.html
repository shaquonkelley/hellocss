When life hands you lemons; fashion.

A story.

Passion: strong and barely controllable emotion.
Design has always been a favorite past time of mine. From interior decorating to recipe development, I've always had a uthe need to express my creativity.
At the age of 11, I was introduced to the Style Network. Every. Single. Day I would spend hours looking at different shows and critiquing looks. I knew then that my heart was in fashion.
I knew that fashion was what I wanted to do, I spent hours at a time, sketching and making garnments with old clothes. Years passed and I had lost touch with my passion. I allowed my doubts intefere with what brung me much joy and excitement. I allowed fear to bargain an uneven trade.
Time has come and gone but the opportunity still remains. This journey is personal.
